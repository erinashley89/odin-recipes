Banana Bread

This recipe is from here.

It calls for an 8 x 4inch loaf pan. If you are using an 8 1/2 x 4 pan, bake for 47-57 minutes. If you are using a 9 x 5 inch pan, bake for 45-55 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 medium very ripe bananas (about 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups mashed)
  • 1/3 cup butter, unsalted or salted, melted
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease the pan.
  2. Mash the bananas and add the butter.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour batter into pan and bake for 55 to 65 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
