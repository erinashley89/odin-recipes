Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 6 tbsp. cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp. vinegar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups water

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 9x13 pan. In a mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, and cocoa. In a second bowl combine the oil, vinegar, vanilla, and water. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet and mix well. Pour into the pan and bake at 350 degrees until the cake is firm in the center and begins to pull away from pan, about 35-40 minutes.
