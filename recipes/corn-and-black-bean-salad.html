Corn and Black Bean Salad

A healthy vegan, southwestern salad!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Tbs. oil
  • 3 Tbs. white balsamic vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. honey
  • 1 Tbs. Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. chili powder
  • 2 lbs. tomatoes, cut into 3/4" pieces, drained
  • 2 cans (15.5 oz. each) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (11 oz.) Mexicorn, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/3 cup sliced fresh basil

In large serving bowl, whisk oil, vinegar, honey, mustard, salt, and chili powder until blended and thickened. Add tomatoes, beans, corn and onion; toss.

Cover and refrigerate, stirring occasionally until chilled, at least 1 hour. Just before serving, gently stir in basil.
